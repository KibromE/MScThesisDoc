\chapter*{Abstract}
\addcontentsline{toc}{chapter}{Abstract}
This thesis is presents a continuous control set model predictive control of Outputs DC voltage
and harmonic of voltage source rectifer under unbalanced and distorted grid voltages. The
proposed control system solves increase in voltage ripple and input current harmonics of voltage
source rectifier exhibit when the supply AC voltage are unbalanced and distorted. In this
paper a mathematical model of the VSR under unbalanced and distorted supply in the form of
positive and negative sequence components, then the model is used to predict the future system
behaviour until the time horizon. A cost function that discribe the desired system performance
is designed,and it is minimized to generate optimal control signal for predefned time horizon.
Lastly the paper discusses the use of CCS-MPC in VSR and presents the obtained result from
MATLAB simulation.\par
\bigskip

\textbf{Keywords: Active Rectifier, VSR, Continuous Control Set (CCS), MPC, Unbalanced
Operating Conditions}
